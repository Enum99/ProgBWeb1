A More Connected World

November 2nd, 2022 by Shak

When computers first started coming into public use many predicitions about how they would shape the future were made. The big one that was everywhere in the late 20th centuries' media was AI. This included everything from HAL 9000 to the Terminator. However 20 years into the 21st century even though AI is making massive strides it is still a long way off. But something that has been making frankly ludicrous strides is communication

Good Communication is the bridge between confusion and clarity

Nat Turner

Information age

The period of history we were born into is accuratly named the Information Age, where we've seen a shift from traditional industry that came about during the industrial revolution to a global economy based upon information Technology

How did this come about

There are many inventions which have been associated with the Information Age however the big three are:

  • The creation of the transistor in 1947 the most basic building block of all modern electronics
  • The optical amplifer in 1957 paving the way for long-distance fiber optic communications
  • Finally the Unix time which synchronizes all computers connected the the internet

Mobile Phones

With the wide adoption of mobile phones and mobile data in every day life we are never more than one metre away from a device that can let us instantly communciate to someone the other side of the globe. And with this communication comes the ability to share information, what once took a trip to the library can now be completed in seconds.

Conclusion

It would be foolish to not acknowledge that this interconnectivity can be exploited by coperations as well as corrupt governments for their own nefarious needs. However, the benefits far outway the negatives, we live in an age where the average person can access information on any subject they want for free. The internet is still young so as long as we raise it right, we'll be creating the most powerful tool humanity has ever wielded.
