What is Computer Science?

"Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems" - University of Maryland

As computer scientists we are forced to think and find creative solutions to problems by thinking how a computer approaches a problem. We plan the appropriate algorithm to solve the problem thinking about the time complexity, hardware capabilities and memory space. It is the job of a computer science to constantly learn and adapt to constantly changing discoveries in the field.
